A way of thinking differently about what design can do — well beyond fixing your process.

I'm a product design leader, strategist and coach. For the past thirteen years I've shipped consumer and enterprise software end-to-end — across agencies, accelerators, scaling startups and large product companies — and built the practices and teams behind it.

Most of the work I'm proudest of lives outside the standard process or framework. It sits in the seam between strategy and craft, where the real work is finding the sharper question that makes the answer clear. That's the work I want to keep doing, and the work I want to help other designers learn how to do.

Today I split my time between a small number of fractional design partnerships, structured 1:1 coaching with mid-to-senior designers, and the occasional workshop or talk — with a growing focus on where the practice goes next. I write here when I have something I'd be willing to defend in person, and I teach as often as the practice will allow.

I'm based in Toronto. I work remote-first, and I travel for the right room.
